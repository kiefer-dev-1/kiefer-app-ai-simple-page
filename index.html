A few words about kIEFER

kIEFER TEK Ltd. is a manufacturing company of Renewable Power Generation Units with a wide range of activity in photovoltaic technology, wind, small hydroelectric, biogas and "portfolio" projects that ranks us among the leaders in the industry.

We have been active in the field since 2014, having until now constructed projects of all technologies with a total power of more than 350 MW. For the years 2021-2024, we have progressed in contracting constructions with a total capacity of 1 GW, in all RES technologies, while at the same time we are proceeding with the development of 1 GW storage stations throughout the Greek territory.

Covering the entire range of activities in the RES sector, having a license to operate in the Cumulative Representation Body with a power license of 200 MW, while we have already integrated the suitably qualified and qualified staff for the operation as Fo.S.E.

Vision & Business Values

Our vision is sustainable development, and we believe in the necessity for the economy to shift towards energy production from renewable sources.

By undertaking the design and construction of green energy production units, we enhance sustainable development, contributing to the creation of a sustainable energy future. Through our projects, we aim to build trusted relationships with our clients by providing them with high-quality services at competitive prices. Continuously following technological advancements, we strive for ongoing improvement in our performance to achieve excellent business results.

The values that govern our company's operations are high standards of quality and safety, reliability, high investment returns, increased profitability for the customer, competitive results, innovation, and respect for the environment.
